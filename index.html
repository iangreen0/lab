Our Foundation

Three pillars. One path forward.

Every element of Haven House is designed around what actually works in long-term recovery — structure you can rely on, people who understand, and the skills to stand on your own.

Accountability

Structure isn't a punishment — it's the scaffolding that protects your recovery while you're still building it.

  • Daily routines & house expectations
  • Randomized drug & alcohol testing
  • Weekly progress check-ins

Support

Recovery is harder alone. Here you live with people who've walked it, and a team that shows up.

  • Peer mentorship & house leadership
  • Connections to therapy, IOP & 12-step
  • A team available when things get hard

Independence

Sobriety is the beginning. What you build on top of it — work, purpose, self-reliance — is what keeps you free.

  • Employment & resume support
  • Budgeting & life-skills coaching
  • Personalized transition planning

Eligibility & Program Details

Simple requirements. Real commitment.

Haven House isn't for everyone — and that's the point. It's for people ready to do the work in a community that holds them to it.

Who qualifies

  • 18+ adultscommitted to a sober lifestyle.
  • Medically stableand past acute detox.
  • Willing to followthe house structure, testing & curfews.
  • Actively engagedin recovery — therapy, IOP, 12-step, or equivalent.
  • Rehab graduates, referrals & self-referralsall welcome.

What's included

  • Furnished room & all utilities— no hidden costs.
  • 90-day minimum stay— longer highly encouraged.
  • House meetings & peer mentorshipincluded weekly.
  • Employment & life-skills support— resume, budgeting, transit.
  • Transition planningbefore you ever leave.

Why Haven House, why now.

The recovery housing gap in Southeast Michigan is well-documented. Treatment centers discharge residents into environments that too often undermine the work they just completed. Structured sober living is the missing bridge — and a scalable one.

  • Proven resident-pay + referral revenue model with predictable occupancy.
  • Asset-light: leased single-family homes, replicable per-house playbook.
  • Deep referral pipeline already established across Oakland County.
  • Mission + margin: outcomes-driven operations, not a numbers game.
  • Clear 3-year expansion roadmap — home 2 and 3 identified.
